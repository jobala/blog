Sinner's Despair

Oh! That stubborn sin,
Against which I cannot win,
Thirsting for the devil’s grin,
I found comfort in the bottle of gin.

Not doing what I ought,
A sickness that my heart caught,
All the goodness that my spirit sought,
My flesh has made naught.

Having fought and failed,
And my path to hades trailed,
With my soul has the serpent sailed,
And the Lord’s mercy paled.
